"Universe Storyboard"

Welcome to my Chemistry page. For this project we had to tell the story of how the universe started using a storyboard. We had to include:

  1. Use and cite 4-5 pieces of relevant evidence
  2. A cited/bibliography page
  3. Clearly integrate both SEPs targeted
  4. Visually appealing and creatively communicate and tell the story
  5. Include 1-2 different cultural origin stories
  6. Include remaining unanswered scientific questions you still have even after researching
